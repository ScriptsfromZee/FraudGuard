**Jojo is a free AI-powered assistant that helps Nigerians identify financial scams in plain language — no sign-up, no payment, no technical knowledge required.**

**Try Jojo at(https://bestfraudguard.streamlit.app)**

## What is Jojo?

Nigeria lost over **₦9.5 billion** to technology-enabled fraud in 2023. Criminal networks 
are now using AI tools — deepfake videos of celebrities, voice cloning software that 
impersonates bank officials and AI-generated fake documents — to target ordinary 
Nigerians, especially the elderly, market traders and young graduates desperate for work.

Jojo exists to fight back.

Describe or paste any suspicious message, phone call, job offer or investment opportunity 
and Jojo will tell you honestly, in calm, plain Nigerian English whether it looks like 
a scam, what the red flags are and exactly what to do next.


## What Jojo Can Check

- Suspicious WhatsApp and text messages
- Phone calls claiming to be from your bank
- Job offers that ask for upfront payments
- Investment platforms promising impossible returns
- Celebrity videos endorsing financial schemes
- Any request to send money urgently


## How It Works

Open the link. Type or paste what you received. Jojo responds immediately.

No account. No download. No fee. Ever.

## About This Project

Jojo was created as part of the **AISIP Cohort 1 AI programme**. It is powered by Groq AI and built specifically for the Nigerian context — it knows GTBank, Zenith, Access, UBA, CBN, NIBSS, BVN, EFCC and the real patterns that Nigerian scammers use.

This repository is the home of the Jojo project. The codebase is not open for 
reuse or redistribution.

*No conversations are stored. No personal data is collected. Jojo exists to help — nothing else.*
